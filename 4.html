Deficiency Symptoms of Essential Elements
•
Nitrogen is one of the important essential elements. Deficiency of nitrogen leads to yellowing of
leaves.
•
Phosphorus is another element of which deficiency will lead to burnt leaf tips and yellowing of the
tips.
•
Potassium deficiency causes interveinal chlorosis and older leaves to wilt.
•
Calcium deficiency leads to blossom end rot.
•
Magnesium causes older leaves to become yellow, that is, chlorosis of leaf due to degradation of
chlorophyll.
