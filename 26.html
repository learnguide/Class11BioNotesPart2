13.9 Photorespiration:
Also known as
photosynthetic carbon oxidation cycle or C2 cycle or Glycolate metabolism.
It is the light dependent utilization of oxygen and reduce CO
2
by photosynthetic organs.
It occurs at high temperature and high Oxygen.
Under this condition the affinity of RuBP towards oxygen increases and CO
2

Photorespiration does not produce energy and it may reduce photosynthesis by up to 50%.
