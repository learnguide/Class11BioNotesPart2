Adrenal gland
It is a pair of gland located in the anterior part of the kidney. It is composed of two types of tissues- outer adrenal
cortex and inner medulla. Adrenal medulla produces two hormones known as
adrenaline and noradrenaline
.
Adrenaline is also known as
emergency hormone
as it is secreted at the time of fear, fight, or flight. Adrenaline
dilates the pupil increases the alertness, sweating etc.
