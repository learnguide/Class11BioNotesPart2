Quantitative comparison between the growths of living systems can be made in 2 ways- absolute growth rate and
relative growth rate.
Absolute growth rate-It is the measurement and comparison of total growth per unit time.
Relative growth rate- It is the growth of the given systems per unit time expressed on a common basis.
E.g.: per unit initial parameter.
