The matured cells gradually get differentiated into permanent tissues by attaining their maximum size in terms of
wall thickening and protoplasmic modifications.
The time interval from the meristematic phase to maturation phase is called
Grand period of growth.
15.1.4 Growth rates:
The expression of increased growth per unit time is called growth rate. The expression of
growth rate mathematically is called arithmetic growth and geometrically is called geometrical growth.
