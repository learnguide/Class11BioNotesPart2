Types of neurons
Multipolar neurons
are those neurons that with one axon and two or more dendrites. For example, in cerebral
cortex.
Bipolar neurons
are with one axon and one dendrite. For example, retina.
Unipolar neurons
are with one axon, no dendrite. For example, embryonic stage.
